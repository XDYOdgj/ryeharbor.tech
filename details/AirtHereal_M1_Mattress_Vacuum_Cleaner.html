AIRTHEREAL M1 Mattress Vacuum Cleaner

A 6-in-1 vacuum designed for deep cleaning your mattress, carpets, and other surfaces. Combines powerful suction, high-frequency vibration, UV-C sterilization, and smart dust detection for a cleaner, healthier home.

Exceptional Suction Power

Equipped with a 14 KPa suction and 400W motor, the AIRTHEREAL M1 delivers strong performance for removing dirt, dust, and allergens from your mattress and throughout your home. Whether you're dealing with a deep-seated mess or a light clean, this vacuum handles it all with ease.

Advanced Multi-Function Technology

This 6-in-1 vacuum features high-frequency vibration, UVC sterilization, and built-in heating to enhance cleaning effectiveness. The ultrasonic technology helps lift dirt from surfaces, while the UVC light kills bacteria and the heating function eliminates dust mites, making your space safer and more hygienic.

Smart Dust Detection System

The AIRTHEREAL M1 includes a dust sensor that automatically adjusts suction power. In Auto mode, the "Dust Mite" indicator remains blue until it detects dust, at which point it turns red and boosts suction. Once the area is clean, the light returns to blue, ensuring energy efficiency and optimal performance.

Portable & User-Friendly Design

Weighing only 4.85 lbs (2.2 kg), this vacuum is lightweight and easy to maneuver. Its 16.4 ft (5 m) power cord provides extended reach, and the two adjustable suction settings (MAX and AUTO) offer flexibility for different cleaning tasks. Ideal for quick, hassle-free cleaning sessions.

Efficient Dust Collection

With a 0.25L (approximately 0.07 gallons) dust bin, the AIRTHEREAL M1 is easy to empty and maintain. This capacity is perfect for cleaning up to four bedrooms, making it an excellent choice for regular maintenance and deep cleaning routines.

Top Choice for Homeowners

Whether you're looking to refresh your mattress, clean your carpets, or maintain your upholstery, the AIRTHEREAL M1 is a versatile, high-performance vacuum that simplifies your cleaning process. Its combination of features and user-friendly design makes it a must-have for any clean-conscious home.
