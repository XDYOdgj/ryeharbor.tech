Its high-frequency roller brush spins over 10,000 times per minute, effectively loosening and extracting stubborn debris – making your home breathe easier and reducing sneezing and coughing.

One-Push 3-in-1 Deep Cleaning Power

With a simple one-touch activation, this handheld vacuum cleaner combines UV light, high heating, and strong suction in one efficient mode, eliminating up to 99.9% of allergens and pet hair.
