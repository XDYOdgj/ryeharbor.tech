Easy Installation Guide

Replace your Bissell vacuum belt with ease by following these simple steps: Use a Philips head screwdriver to remove the screws and lift off the brush plate. Take out the old belt from the roller. Attach the new belt to the motor pulley and then slide it onto the roller. Make sure the belt is properly seated and the roller is aligned correctly. Replace the brush plate and secure it with the screws. If the belt feels too tight during installation, pause and adjust, as improper tension can lead to damage or poor performance.

Regular Replacement for Optimum Efficiency

To maintain the efficiency and longevity of your Bissell vacuum, it's recommended to replace the vacuum belt every 3 to 6 months. Over time, the belt can wear down due to frequent use, which may lead to reduced suction or complete belt failure. Tecreddy offers a reliable solution with a package containing 4 replacement belts, ensuring you have enough on hand for regular maintenance and extended use.
