Cen-Tec Systems 93620 Low Voltage Central Vacuum Hose - Light Gray

The Cen-Tec Systems 93620 is a reliable and user-friendly vacuum hose designed for seamless integration with most central vacuum systems. With a 1.375-inch diameter and 35 feet of length, it offers extended reach and superior coverage, making cleaning every corner of your home effortless, even with furniture in the way.

Equipped with a 360-degree swivel handle, this hose provides precise control over your vacuum tools, ensuring smooth movement and reducing strain during cleaning. The soft, flexible surface with rounded corrugations is gentle on floors and surfaces, eliminating the need for a hose sock to protect your home interiors.

Built with a lightweight and crush-proof construction, the 93620 is easy to maneuver, helping you maintain your routine without fatigue. Its design also features a locking stub tube that keeps your wand securely in place, preventing accidental disconnection and ensuring uninterrupted cleaning sessions.

The hose is compatible with a wide range of central vacuum brands, including Eureka, Beam, Electrolux, Vacumaid, Canavac, Hoover, Cyclovac, Husky, and Vacuflo. It works with both pin jack and M+S inlets, making it a versatile choice for many home setups.

Thanks to its low voltage rating, this hose is not designed for use with electric power nozzles. However, it works perfectly with standard central vacuum systems, offering a safe and trouble-free operation.

The pearl gray finish is not only stylish but also designed to prevent scuffing on walls, floors, and other surfaces. Its construction ensures durability without compromising comfort or functionality, making it a perfect fit for your daily cleaning needs.

Whether you're maintaining your home or tackling tough messes, the 93620 vacuum hose is built to simplify your life. Experience the convenience of a premium, yet affordable, central vacuum solution today.
