Featuring high-quality special rubber, this vacuum belt is built for long-lasting performance. The unique surface lines enhance friction, helping the belt grip better and maintain smooth operation. Designed with original Bissell models in mind, our belt fits perfectly and ensures seamless integration with your vacuum cleaner.

Replace your vacuum belt with ease. Simply use a Phillips head screwdriver to remove the screws and lift off the brush plate. Take out the old belt and install the new one around the motor pulley and roller. Once in place, slide the roller back into the unit and ensure it's positioned correctly. Reattach the brush plate and tighten the screws. If the belt feels too tight during installation, stop and check before proceeding.
