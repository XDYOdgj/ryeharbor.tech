Saerke W07 Portable Carpet Cleaner Machine – Powerful & Versatile

Experience a hassle-free way to clean your carpets, upholstery, and more with the Saerke W07. This powerful vacuum cleaner is designed to make deep cleaning efficient and effective, whether you're dealing with pet stains, smoke odors, or tough spills.

Unmatched Suction Power

The Saerke W07 is equipped with a high-performance 450W brushless motor that delivers over 11 kPa of suction power. This ensures that even the deepest stains and embedded dirt are removed with ease, leaving your surfaces clean and fresh.

Portable & Easy to Use

Featuring an ultra-long 3.93 ft hose and a 16.4 ft power cord, the Saerke W07 offers maximum flexibility for cleaning across different areas of your home. Its lightweight design and convenient storage solutions, such as built-in wire and hose brackets and a dual brush hook, make it ideal for everyday use and space-saving storage.

Large & Upgraded Water Tanks

With two large transparent water tanks—one for clean water (50 oz) and one for dirty water (29 oz)—the Saerke W07 allows for more uninterrupted cleaning with less frequent emptying. This enhances your cleaning efficiency and saves valuable time.

Perfect for Pet Owners

Whether it's pet urine, muddy paws, or stubborn pet stains, the Saerke W07 is designed to tackle them all. It effectively removes tough odors and leaves your home smelling clean and fresh, offering peace of mind for pet parents.

Versatile for Multiple Surfaces

The Saerke W07 is a multi-purpose cleaning tool that can be used on a wide range of fabric surfaces, including carpets, sofas, area rugs, mattresses, and car seats. With two interchangeable brush heads, you can handle different types of stains and accidents with ease, ensuring a thorough and satisfying cleaning experience every time.
