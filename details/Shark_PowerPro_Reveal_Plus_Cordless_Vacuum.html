Shark PowerPro Reveal Plus Cordless Vacuum

Effortless cleaning with advanced technology for your home and kitchen.

Say goodbye to dusty corners and stubborn messes with the Shark PowerPro Reveal Plus Cordless Vacuum. This powerful vacuum is designed to tackle every type of floor effortlessly, whether it's carpet, hardwood, or tile. With intelligent FloorDetect Technology, it automatically adapts to your floor type and adjusts brushroll speed—no more switching nozzles or settings required.

The Dirt-Reveal Technology provides a clear view of hidden debris, ensuring thorough cleaning and minimal effort. A built-in LED headlight helps illuminate dark corners, making it easier to spot and remove dust and small particles, even in hard-to-reach areas.

This vacuum is especially effective for pet owners, thanks to its no-hair-wrap brushroll that ensures powerful pet hair pickup without getting clogged. Experience smarter, cleaner living with the Shark PowerPro Reveal Plus Cordless Vacuum.
